Toilet Paper Mummy

Supplies

  • Rolls of toilet paper
    • Typically one to two per person

Instructions

  1. Rules
    • Break the participants up into even groups or teams. Sizes will vary depending on the number of people playing
    • Each of the teams will pick someone to be a mummy, and all the other team members will grab a roll of toilet paper
    • When the signal is given, each of the teams will begin wrapping their mummy in toilet paper
  2. Winner
    • Option one for a winner is the team who uses all of their toilet paper first
    • Option two is the team who covers their mummy entirely first
